Nino Espressino, once the most beloved espresso dealer in the noir alleys of Espressia, now finds himself racing against time. His rival, the milk-spiking menace Latte Luchador, has kidnapped his biscuit companion Bambino Biscottino. From the smoke-filled jazz cafés of Moka Metropolis to the espresso-stained ruins of the Forgotten Roastery, Nino must grind through waves of bean-thugs and defeat notorious café bosses. His journey is a blend of revenge, crema, and redemption.
